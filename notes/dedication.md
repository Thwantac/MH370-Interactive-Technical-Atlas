# Dedication

This project is dedicated in part to the memory of Geoffrey Thomas, respected Australian aviation journalist, aviation-safety commentator, and longtime voice in public aviation education.

His work, including the now-closed Daily Airline News podcast, helped make complex aviation issues accessible to a wider audience.

The MH370 Interactive Technical Atlas is built in that same spirit of curiosity, careful source-tracing, and respect for aviation history, safety, and the people affected by unresolved aviation events.
